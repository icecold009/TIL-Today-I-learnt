# TIL Template

Use this template for new notes so the repo stays easy to scan and easier to cross-link over time.

```md
---
title: "Short title"
tags:
  - topic
  - subtopic
difficulty: beginner
related:
  - ./related-note.md
---

# Short title

## What it is
Give the one-sentence definition.

## Why it matters
Explain the practical reason to care.

## Pitfalls
List the common misunderstandings or edge cases.

## Example
Show a small example, analogy, or diagram.

## Related notes
Link to nearby notes that reinforce the same idea.
```

Keep the note concise. A good TIL should usually fit on one screen, with links doing the heavy lifting.
